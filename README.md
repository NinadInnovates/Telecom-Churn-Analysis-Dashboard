# Telecom-Churn-Analysis-Dashboard

1. Short Description

An interactive customer churn analysis dashboard built using Microsoft Power BI to analyze telecom customer behavior, identify churn patterns, and measure revenue loss.The dashboard provides insights into customer demographics, service usage, contract types, and geographic churn trends to help businesses improve customer retention strategies.

2. Objectives

Identify customer churn trends

Analyze churn rate across demographics

Understand factors influencing churn

Monitor revenue loss due to churn

Provide actionable insights for customer retention strategies

3. Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technolohies:

Microsoft Power BI

Power Query – Data cleaning and transformation

DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – KPI and metric calculations

Data Modeling – Relationships and schema design

Data Visualization – Interactive charts and dashboards

4. Features & Highlights

Telecommunication companies often face high customer churn, which leads to significant revenue loss and increased customer acquisition costs.

Without proper data analysis, businesses struggle to understand:

Why customers are leaving?

Which customer segments are most likely to churn?

Which services or pricing plans contribute to churn?

Which geographic regions have the highest churn?

This lack of insight makes it difficult for companies to create effective customer retention strategies.

5. Goal of the Dashboard

The goal of this dashboard is to provide a comprehensive view of customer churn patterns so decision-makers can quickly identify risk areas.

The dashboard helps to:

Monitor overall churn rate and revenue loss

Identify high-risk customer segments

Analyze churn drivers such as contract type, payment method, and internet service

Compare churn across age groups, tenure, and states

Enable data-driven retention strategies

6. Walkthrough of Key Visuals

📌 KPI Summary Cards

The top section of the dashboard provides high-level performance metrics:

Total Customers: 6,396

Active Customers: 4,259

Churned Customers: 1,725

Churn Rate: 27%

Revenue Loss: $3.40M

These KPIs allow executives to quickly understand the scale of churn impact.

7. Business Impact & Key Insights

Overall churn rate is 27%, indicating a significant retention challenge.

Fiber optic users show the highest churn, suggesting possible service issues.

Month-to-month contracts are highly volatile compared to long-term contracts.

New customers (<6 months) are at higher risk of leaving.

💼 Business Impact

This dashboard enables businesses to:

Identify high-risk customer segments

Understand service-level churn drivers

Develop targeted retention campaigns

Improve customer satisfaction and loyalty

Reduce revenue loss from churn

🚀 Strategic Recommendations

Offer discounts for long-term contracts

Improve fiber optic service quality

Create early-stage customer engagement programs

Promote auto-payment methods

Focus retention campaigns in high-churn regions
